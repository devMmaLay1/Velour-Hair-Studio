Our Story

Born from a belief that
every woman deserves luxury.

Velour Hair Studio began in a small studio on Victoria Island in 2020 — just one chair, one stylist, and an unshakeable conviction that Black women in Lagos deserved a salon experience as refined as any in the world.

Our founder, Adaeze Okafor, had spent years watching clients settle for less — rushed appointments, harsh chemicals, and spaces that never quite felt like theirs. She built Velour as the antidote: a place where every detail, from the scent in the air to the products on the shelf, was chosen with intention.

Five years later, Velour has grown to three branches across Lagos and Abuja, a team of fifteen specialist stylists, and a community of over five thousand women who call us their salon home. The chair count has changed. The conviction hasn't.

Demand drives our first expansion — a full-floor studio in Lekki Phase 1 with five styling chairs, a dedicated colour bar, and a team of four specialist stylists.
